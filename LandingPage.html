About Us

Nepal e-Governance is a government initiative aimed at providing efficient and transparent online services to the citizens of Nepal. Our primary focus is on improving the passport application process by eliminating the need to wait in long queues at passport offices.

Our system allows citizens to book passport appointments online and get a token number, which they can use to skip the queue and get their passport processed quickly.
